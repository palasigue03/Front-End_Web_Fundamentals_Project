THE AWARDING

Search for Outstanding Alumni

October 13, 2018

7:00 p.m.

Tarlac State University Alumni Association Inc. & the College of Computer Studies selected one Outstanding Alumni from each of their respected batch — years 2001 to 2017, based on their recent acomplishments throughout the years after graduating. Nominees for the award were nominated by their co-TSU CCSS Alumni between September 1 to September 6, 2018. The awarding will be held during the October 13, 2018 Alumni Symposium & 2nd Alumni Gala Night at the TSU Gymnasium.

The Alumni Symposium and 2nd Alumni Gala Night is open to all bonafide alumni of TSU and TSU-CCS. The event will host performances from the members of TSUAAI. There will also be a ruffle promo. First prize includes 2018 Macbook Pro! and other consolation prizes. See you there ka-CCS!
